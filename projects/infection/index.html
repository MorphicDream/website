Infection

Worldwide game-mode that infects the world block by block

Overview

The idea of the game-mode is that the world has become infected. The infected blocks hurt when stood on and zombies spawns on them and if the blocks are broken silver fish are released.

Platform: Minecraft - Java

Details

There are three phases to this game-mode, in order to keep the game-mode fresh and make the infection slowly become worse. There is a counter to say when blocks will be infected and also what phase is happening at that moment.

Phase one

In this phase the blocks around players will become infected every ten seconds. These blocks dont do much except if broken they will spawn silver fish. Creepers that explode will infect the ground as well.

Phase two

This makes the blocks give a small amount of damage when stood on and still create silver fish when broken. Water becomes infected as well so swimming causes damage. Boats are also effected when in water, after so long they will break.

Phase three

This is where is gets interesting, all the above happens but the damage from blocks is increased and zombies will spawn. The zombies are not effected by daylight. When zombies spawn underwater they will become drowned and when drowned in desert biomes they will become husks.

A general thing across all phases are that any mobs spawned only have a life expectancy of around thirty seconds. When they expire they turn into infected blocks, this includes the silver fish. This was created to stop huge amounts of mobs being spawned in. Although you can set a max spawn limit in the config. There are a few blocks that won't be infected, these are mostly chests, lava, water, obsidian, end portals etc, this is to make sure the game can actually be completed without a critical block becoming infected.

The idea of the game-mode is to get to the end and destroy the dragon. When the dragon is killed the infection stops. The infection also pauses when all players leave the server, this was just in case server owners wanted to run an event and do a phase every day or week.
