Emberlock NPC Generator

NPC Generator used for the podcasts at The Ashen Ink

Overview

This is a generator to make random NPCs based on the fictional city of Emberlock, used in The Ashen Ink podcasts.

Platform: Windows PC

Details

The generator creates a few things randomly, first and last name, eye colour, hair colour, job title, region (which is specific to Emberlock), race, parents and children. I have had plenty of thoughts on updating this to become a normal NPC generator that's not specific to Emberlock, but for now its completed.

The NPCs generated can be saved within and obviously loaded into the app to view them. This seemed like a handy feature to have.
