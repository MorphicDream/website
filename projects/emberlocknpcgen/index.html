Emberlock NPC Generator

NPC Generator used for the podcasts at The Ashen Ink

Overview

This is a generator to make random NPCs based on the fictional city of Emberlock, used in The Ashen Ink podcasts.

Platform: Windows PC

Details

This is a generator based on the fictional city of Emberlock. It will generate a first and last name, eye colour, hair colour, a region within the Emberlock City, a job title based on the region so you wont have a baker in the fishmarket district. It also generates race, and you can adjust the parents and children. There is a save function which allows you to keep any NPCs you might want to use for specific things. My version obviously has the Ashen Ink characters saved within it.

I have had plenty of thoughts on updating this to become a normal NPC generator that's not specific to Emberlock, but I feel like thats just a project growing arms and legs, it does what I wanted it to do and its completed which is always good
