Ashen Ink Podcast

Podcast based on a fictional city called Emberlock

Overview

Storys told about the people that live and work in the fictional city of Emberlock. With glimpses into many different areas in the city.

Platform: Multiple

Details

Originally a single season with ten episodes was planned. The first pilot episode was put on Spotify and YouTube and got some good feedback. I scaled back the number of episodes because at the time i was starting to feel that it was taking too long. But I then decided to take a break from this project until I was in a place that was more comfortable. It also took me longer to find and make sound effects etc. This has slowed things down.

I did design the entire city with all the areas and a rough idea behind the areas to make story telling easier. It's nice to have references when telling tales.

The first episode is based on Joss Lowharrow a fish seller and the story about his silver eyes and a short glimpse into his past. It also hints at a mystery within the waters around Emberlock.

There is a discord for this when i get more episodes done. The discord will allow players to interact with a bot that will allow them little secrets about places and people within Emberlock. Its live at the moment but I want to have more episodes done before releasing the discord link.

The idea is to release an episode every week. So I want to have them basically done and just set to release at specific times.
